Construction Updates

Construction Notice 1/26/2023

Summary

Begining Monday, February 6, and continuing for approximately two weeks, 220th St will be reduced to a single lane in each direction between Meyler Rd and Central Dr from 7am to 5pm, Monday through Friday. This traffic control will support installation of a sound wall on the south side of the Replacement Work Area.

Traffic Control

While 220th St is reduced to a single lane in each direction, flaggers will be posted at each end of the closure to direct traffic.

Pedestrian Access

Pedestrian Access will be maintained along the south side of 220th St. Active crosswalks will be marked with construction signage.
